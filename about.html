About Aanuoluwapo Susan

Creative Content Creator, Event Planner & Shoemaking Expert

My Story

Aanuoluwapo Susan Fawekun is a visionary marketing strategist and content creator based in Ado Ekiti, Nigeria.

With skills honed in shoemaking and training from 2017-2020, Aanuoluwapo is also certified in Project Management. She is self-motivated, dedicated to quality, and passionate about impacting knowledge. Her understanding of Nigerian Labour Law complements her commitment to continuous learning.

Beyond shoemaking, Aanuoluwapo has solid knowledge in content creation, specializing in capturing videos and photos for events, weddings, families, brands, and businesses. Her work blends creativity with technical expertise, turning moments into memorable visual stories. A refined and God-fearing woman, she is committed to empowering businesses through creativity, strategy, and impactful solutions.

As a successful event planner and full-time content creator at Legacy gardens, Atlas motel Ado Ekiti, she has organized numerous successful events, boosting social visibility and engagement for the brand.
